Visit Fayence!

A typical Provencal vilage only an hour from the French Riviera

Top three activities in Fayence:

Stunning views and historic buildings:

Set on the top of the hill at 665m above sea level, you can see the planes taking off in the valley, surrounding villages in the commune or the lake of St. Casien glistening in the summer sun

Weekly market of everyday items:

Visit the weekly market to buy produce directly from the producers as well as locally crafted items for souvenirs.

World class gliding available

Visit the world famous gliding school to take to the skies and view the area from on high, or use it as launch point to have dinner in the Alps.
